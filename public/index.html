  About Us


Trackboard is a simple and technological way of managing and requesting posters online across the universities and institutions. We aim to create a well-structured poster management system for both the administrators and the users.

  • Request and manage poster authorization faster and easier via online
  • No more outdated posters on campus bulletin boards
